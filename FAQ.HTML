FAQ

At The Freethinking School, we encourage your curiosity to understand our methodology better. Please do feel free to call or write to us about any query you may have regarding your child's formative education.

How old should my child be before I obtain admission in the Freethinking School? +
The minimum age for admission is typically 3 years, but it depends on the specific program. Please contact us for more details.
What are the school timings? +
The school runs from 8:30 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday to Friday.
Tell me more about the Freethinking curriculum? +
Our curriculum is focused on critical thinking, creativity, and experiential learning. We combine traditional education with innovative methods.
What are the extracurricular activities? +
We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports, arts, music, drama, and more to ensure holistic development.
Is there a daycare facility after school? +
Yes, we provide a daycare facility for students after school hours.
How is TFS different from other schools? +
TFS focuses on personalized learning, critical thinking, and experiential education, allowing students to actively engage in their learning.
What are the facilities available at TFS? +
TFS provides state-of-the-art classrooms, sports facilities, libraries, art studios, and computer labs to enhance student learning.
What is your student/teacher ratio? +
We maintain a low student-to-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention to each student. The ratio is typically 12:1.
Does the fees include snacks and lunch? +
Yes, the fees cover snacks and lunch provided to all students.
Do you have transport? +
Yes, we provide a school bus service for students who need transportation.
What are the safety measures implemented at the school? +
We implement a range of safety measures including security personnel, CCTV cameras, first-aid facilities, and safety drills.
