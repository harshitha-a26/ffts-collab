Welcome to Freethinking School

Welcome to The Freethinking School, a unique fun-filled space, buzzing with freethinking creativity; a space that inspires a love for learning for the rest of your child's life; a space that your child loves to eagerly come to, every day.

We provide a stimulating environment rich in experiences that helps each child discover their full potential by nurturing creativity, purposeful thinking, and equipping them with the necessary skill sets that contribute towards joyful, productive living.

At The Freethinking School, our focus is on montessori-based, personalized, experiential learning that transcends the classroom, to make every child a freethinker for life.
